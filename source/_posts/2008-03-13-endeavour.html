"The rocket lay on the launching field, blowing out pink clouds of fire and oven heat. The rocket stood in the cold winter morning, masking summer with every breath of its mighty exhausts. The rocket made climates, and summer lay for a brief moment upon the land."

This is Space Shuttle Endeavour OV-105, being lunched for STS-123 mission to the ISS.
